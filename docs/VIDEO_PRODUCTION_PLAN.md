# Video Production Plan

## Format
Product story video — 75–90 seconds.
Half problem, half working product.

## Title
**SponsorLens — Stop Applying Blindly**

---

## Storyboard

### 0–8 sec — Hook
**Visual:** Student opens 10 browser tabs — Handshake, Workday, LinkedIn applications.
**Voiceover:** "International students don't just search for jobs. They search for jobs they're actually allowed to pursue."
**Text overlay:** "40 minutes per application. One hidden sentence can make it impossible."

### 8–20 sec — Problem
**Visual:** Zoom into job descriptions. Highlight phrases:
- "U.S. citizens only"
- "No sponsorship"
- "Must be permanently authorized"
- "Security clearance required"

**Voiceover:** "Job boards may show sponsorship fields, but those fields can be self-reported, ambiguous, or contradicted by the job description itself."
**Text overlay:** "Checkboxes are not enough."

### 20–35 sec — Product Reveal
**Visual:** Click the SponsorLens extension icon in Chrome toolbar.
**Voiceover:** "I built SponsorLens — a local-first Chrome extension that reads visible job postings and turns confusing authorization language into a clear decision."
**Text overlay:** "Strong Fit / Risky Fit / Low Fit"

### 35–55 sec — Demo Sequence
**Visual:** Three quick demos using Demo Mode buttons.

**Demo 1 — Strong Fit:**
Green card → 95/100 → "OPT candidates welcome, CPT eligible"
→ "Apply and tailor your application."

**Demo 2 — Risky Fit:**
Orange card → 62/100 → "must be authorized to work"
→ "Ask the recruiter to clarify first."

**Demo 3 — Low Fit:**
Red card → 12/100 → "U.S. citizens only, security clearance"
→ "Skip. Time saved: 40 minutes."

**Voiceover:** "SponsorLens detects positive signals, hard restrictions, ambiguous wording, and contradictions. Then it explains what to do next."

### 55–70 sec — Killer Feature
**Visual:** Scroll to Recruiter Message Studio. Switch between Short / Polite / Cover Letter variants. Click Copy.
**Voiceover:** "It also provides recruiter-safe messages, so students can ask better questions without overpromising or sounding desperate."
**Text overlay:** "Ask before wasting 40 minutes."

### 70–82 sec — Privacy & Credibility
**Visual:** Show README on GitHub, test results in terminal, privacy documentation.
**Voiceover:** "The MVP runs locally, collects no resumes, no immigration documents, and no government IDs. It is decision support, not legal advice."
**Text overlay:** "Local-first. Privacy-first. Not legal advice."

### 82–90 sec — Closing
**Visual:** Landing page hero / GitHub repo.
**Voiceover:** "SponsorLens helps international students stop applying blindly. It doesn't replace legal advice. It replaces wasted time with clarity."
**Text overlay:** "SponsorLens — Apply smarter as an international student."

## Recording Tips

- Use 1080p or higher
- Chrome at 100% zoom, clean profile (no other extensions visible)
- Use Demo Mode for deterministic, reproducible results
- Record voiceover separately for better audio quality
- Keep transitions clean — simple cuts, no fancy effects
- Add captions/subtitles for accessibility
