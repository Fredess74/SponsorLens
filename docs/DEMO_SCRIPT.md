# SponsorLens Demo Script

## 30-second version
“I’m an international student on F-1. The hardest part of job search isn’t finding jobs—it’s knowing which ones are actually worth applying to. SponsorLens reads visible job language, flags work-authorization friction and contradictions, and gives a clear Strong Fit, Risky Fit, or Low Fit recommendation. It’s local-first and privacy-first, with no resumes or immigration documents collected.”

## 60-second version
“I’m an international student on F-1. For students like me, the hardest part of job search is not just finding jobs. It is knowing which jobs are actually worth applying to. Job boards may show sponsorship fields, but those are often self-reported, ambiguous, or contradicted by the posting text. SponsorLens is a Chrome extension that reads visible job text and translates it into a clear fit decision—Strong, Risky, or Low—based on sponsorship and work-authorization language, contradiction detection, and optional profile matching for CPT/OPT/STEM OPT paths. It also suggests a recruiter-safe clarification message so students can ask better questions before spending 40 minutes on an application.”

## 3-minute version
### Opening
“I’m an international student on F-1. For students like me, the hardest part of job search is not just finding jobs. It is knowing which jobs are actually worth applying to.”

### Problem
“Job boards can show sponsorship fields, but they can be ambiguous, self-reported, or contradicted by the actual job description. Students can spend hours applying to roles that were never realistic.”

### Solution
“SponsorLens reads the visible posting, detects work-authorization friction, matches it against the student’s profile, and gives Strong Fit / Risky Fit / Low Fit. It does this locally in the extension with no backend and no external API.”

### Demo sequence
1. **Strong demo:** show OPT/CPT-friendly language and explain positive signals.
2. **Risky demo:** show ambiguous authorization language and explain recruiter clarification recommendation.
3. **Low demo:** show citizens-only / clearance / no sponsorship language and highlight time-saved estimate.
4. **Contradiction explanation:** demonstrate “visa sponsorship available” with “no visa sponsorship” and show contradiction warning.

### Closing
“SponsorLens does not replace legal advice. It replaces wasted time with clarity.”
