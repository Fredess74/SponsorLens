SponsorLens

Apply smarter as an international student.

SponsorLens is a local-first Chrome extension that helps F-1, CPT, OPT, and STEM OPT students analyze job postings for hidden work-authorization friction before spending 40 minutes on applications.

International students don’t need more job listings. They need clarity.

Job boards can show sponsorship fields, but those fields are often self-reported, ambiguous, incomplete, or contradicted by the job description.

Hidden restrictions

“No sponsorship”, “U.S. citizens only”, “security clearance required”.

Ambiguous wording

“Must be authorized to work in the U.S.” may need recruiter clarification.

Wasted time

Students can spend 30–60 minutes on applications that were never realistic.

SponsorLens adds the missing decision layer.

  1. Open a job posting.
  2. Click SponsorLens.
  3. The extension reads visible job text locally.
  4. It detects work-authorization signals and contradictions.
  5. It returns Strong Fit, Risky Fit, or Low Fit.
  6. It suggests a recruiter-safe clarification message.
Job Posting → SponsorLens Analysis → Apply / Clarify / Skip

Product Preview

Strong Fit

OPT/CPT-friendly language found. No hard restriction detected.

Risky Fit

Ambiguous authorization language. Clarify before completing a full application.

Low Fit

Hard restriction detected: U.S. citizens only / security clearance.

Recruiter-safe message

“Could you confirm whether candidates with F-1 OPT/CPT work authorization are considered?”

Job-board checkboxes are useful. SponsorLens goes further.

  • Checks the language behind the checkbox
  • Flags contradictions
  • Personalizes by CPT / OPT / STEM OPT profile
  • Gives next-step messaging, not just raw data
  • Works across job pages beyond one platform

Built for trust from day one.

  • Local-first analysis
  • No resumes collected
  • No immigration documents collected
  • No government IDs collected
  • No external API calls in MVP
  • Optional profile stored locally only
  • Not legal or immigration advice
